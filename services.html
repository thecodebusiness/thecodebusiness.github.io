Services

We’re a full-service development and consulting firm.

We listen first, collaborate with each other in person, strive for simplicity, and build for the future. In addition to custom app development we offer a range of other services to support your project from start to finish.

What Can We Help?

Custom Web Development

We have been using Django and Flask to build web applications for over 5 years now. We use AngularJS and VueJS to build web applications. We have worked with Bootstrap, Wordpress and Shopify.

Mobile App Development

React Native allows us to have a single codebase for both Android and iOS applications. It means our team can deliver features faster while reusing most of the components for both the platforms.

Discovery Workshop

Your project doesn't have to feel complicated. Clarify the vision and build a shared understanding among stakeholders about what is being built, for whom, and the best way to build it.

DevOps

It doesn't matter if you're deploying a new app or a new feature, speed and stability are everything. At TCB, we build and maintain stable server environments that provide you with consistent delivery to your users.

Managed Hosting & Backups

TCB provides ongoing support and maintenance of your cloud-based application servers, including hardware and software setup and configuration, technical support, patch management, system maintenance, and monitoring.

Upgrades

Continually improve your software products in areas of design and user experience by staying up to date. It’s imperative to think about both the front end and the back end simultaneously in order to keep your website running smoothly.
